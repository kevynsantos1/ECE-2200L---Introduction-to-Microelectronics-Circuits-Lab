Our objective during this lab is to analyze the current-voltage relationship of 
two semiconductor PN diodes, one will be a Silicon diode and the other will be a 
Germanium diode. We also want to determine the reverse saturation current and diode 
ideality factor from the I-V characteristic plot from our gathered data. We will 
begin by observing the forward-biased relationship between the voltage over the diode 
for both diodes vs. various current values. We will get these measurements directly. 
Then we will observe the reverse-biased relationship between the voltage and the 
current over the diode. We will calculate current by getting the voltage across a 100k 
resistor, and then dividing the voltage by that resistance value to get current. Lastly,
we will plot all of our graphs, and compare our data.
