In this lab experiment our objective is to analyze the change in output voltage (Vout) 
and phase angle (θ) at different frequencies. We will use Excel and PSpice to determine 
our theoretical values. Then we will compare the theoretical values to our experimental 
values. At the end we will plot 20log(Vout/Vs) vs. phase angle using the experimental, 
theoretical, and simulation values. Our goal is to confirm the relation of frequency to 
output voltage and phase angle in an RC circuit.
