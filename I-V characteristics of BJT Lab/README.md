Our objective during this lab is to study the current-voltage relationships of an NPN bipolar junction transistor. 
For our pre-lab we will simulate the circuit in figure 3 and gather our simulation data. In the first part of our lab 
we will be measuring IB versus VBE at VCE = 1V, then we will plot IB vs. VBE. In the next part of our lab we will capture 
IC vs VCE curves at step values of 20µA, 29µA, and 40µA. In the last part of our lab we will bias a BJT circuit with four 
resistors and measure the DC value, then we will compare the values to the ones we got in the pre-lab.
